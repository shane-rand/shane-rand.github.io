Discover Credit Card Redesign

The goal of this project for Discover was to completely overhaul its 4 main credit card pages, which range from a generic Discover it card to more specific Student, Travel, and NHL Cards.

All 4 pages share similar design elements, with a standard marquee + card rotation to kick off the page.

After some in depth information, a large mid-page call out asks the user to compare Discover's cards with other major brands. This crafty module uses css gradients to create a fade out, and the chart is active html. Discover supports IE7, and so images were used in place of the css gradients to achieve a similar effect.

These pages are completely responsive, and are 100% ADA compliant for users with disabilities.
